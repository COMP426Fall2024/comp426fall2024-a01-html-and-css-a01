Stage 2: The Primal Period

Following the Creation Period, history then moves into the Primal Period. The Primal Period serves as the historical time span where the Pokemon world began to take on its physical features. The first three Pokemon during this age are Kyogre, Groudon, and Rayquaza. Kyogre was formed by the pressure of the deep ocean and would fill the seas. Groudon was formed by from the magma of Earth's core and created landmasses. Lastly, Rayquaza was formed by the minerals in the Ozone layer and soared the skies.

After the landmasses had been formed by Groudon, a giant Pokemon named Regigigas would move the continents around. These continents would be shifted around to form the nine regions we would experience throughout the pokemon games. When rearraning these landmasses, Stark mountain was created and Heatran was made. Heatran, the Lava Pokemon, is the only Pokemon who can control the lava flow throughout the Earth.

Then, Regigigas would create three titans, in his own image, to usher in the ice, stone, and iron ages: Regice, Regirock, and Registeel. During these ages, these titan Pokemon were created to assist would human and Pokemon development around the world. After these ages concluded, Regigias, Regice, Regirock, and Registeel would all go away into hiding as development was now sufficient on its own.
