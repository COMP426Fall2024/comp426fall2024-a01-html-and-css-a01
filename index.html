What is the Pokemon Universe?

As of August 2021, the Pokemon franchise was estimated to be worth $105 Billion. This multi-billion dollar franchise provided hours upon hours of childhood fun for many kids (and even adults) around the globe. Since the first Pokémon game was released in 1996, there have been 40 "canonical" games that have added to the history of the Pokémon universe. After 28 years of games, the Pokémon universe has been compiled into this complex series of events that now accounts for the history of the Pokémon universe. From creating time and space, forming the planet, the beginning of the Pokémon race, and more, the Pokémon universe has a very rich history.
