In the beginning, there was nothing. In a void of nothing, there appeared an egg. This egg would soon hatch to be the first Pokemon and what many would call "The Original One": Arceus. Arceus was the first Pokemon and whom many consider to be the most powerful Pokemon ever. According to Pokemon Mythology, Arceus created the Pokemon Universe using its 1,000 arms.

After Arceus created the universe, they created three new Pokemon: Palkia, Dialga, and Giratina. Arceus assiged Palkia to control space and assigned Dialga to control time. Additionally, Arceus assigned Giratina to control antimatter. However, Arceus realized that Giratina was too dangerous and violent, so Arceus banished Giratina to the reverse side of the Pokemon world, the Distortion World.

As Arceus had created a Universe with Time, Space and Antimatter, Arceus began to design the blueprint for ordinary Pokemon. Arceus thereafter created Azelf, Uxie, and Mesprit so they could create willpower, knowledge, and emotion, respectively. These are the attributes that would be in all humans and Pokemon alike. Once Lake Valor, Lake Acuity, and Lake Verity was created, Azel, Uxie, and Mesprit dove to the bottom of the Lakes and resided there, respectively.

Once willpower, knowledge, and emotions were created by Azel, Uxie, and Mesprit, Mew was created. Mew contains DNA from every single pokemon, so many believe that Mew is the common ancestor from all Common Pokemon. This was the beginning of the Pokemon race, and the Pokemon Universe was effectively underway.
