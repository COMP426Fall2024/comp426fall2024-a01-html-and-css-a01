Stage 3: The Darkest Day

Home The Creation The Primal Period Recent Past Contemporary History

20,000 years prior to modern day, a metoreorite carrying the Pokemon Eternatus hit planet Earth. When Eternatus hit planet Earth, Eternatus began to go on a rampage and destroy everything around it. The particles released by Eternatus would infuse surrounding Pokemon promoting the concept of "Dynamaxing." This caused Pokemon to become massive in size and cause massive destruction.

As the region where Eternatus landed would fall into chaos, it would take two heroic Pokemon to pull the region back into stability. The legendary Pokemon Zamazenta and Zacian would defeat Eternatus with an ancient Sword and Shield, respectively, and forced Eternatus into an inactive state. This event would go down as "The Darkest Day" in Pokemon history as the world as everybody knew it, nearly faded into uncontrollable chaos.

After Eternatus was defeated, Zamazenta and Zacian would fade away as legends and wishing stars would scatter throughout the region. These wishing stars were essential for people being able to control the phenomenon known as "Dynamaxing." After "The Darkest Day" concluded and Pokemon reverted back to order and stability, the Pokemon universe would enter time of piece and prosperity for thousands of years.
