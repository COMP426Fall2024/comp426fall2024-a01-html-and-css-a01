Our Contemporary History begins with our first Pokemon generation games, so Pokemon Red & Blue, for example. At this point in Pokemon history, we begin seeing major antagonists form from selfish and nefarious means. For instance, this is the point in time where Mewtwo is created in a lab from Mew and wreaks havoc upon the Kanton region. Then a few of years later, we see organizations named Team Aqua and Team Magma re-awaken Groudon and Kyogre, bringing the Hoenn region back into disorder.

Then, we see years later something similar: an organization named Team Galactic would summon Dialga, Palkia, and Giratina. Where Giratina comes from the Distortion World, this summoning caused major rips in space-time. Following this crisis, we would see bedlam in the Unova region where Team Plasma would awaken two legendary dragons named Zekrom and Reshiram. Team Plasma would eventually try to take over the Unova Region with these Pokemon, but would ultimately fail.

As years would continue, we would see similar-minded groups try to utilize Legendary Pokemon for nefarious means. Team Flare would attempt to use Xerneas's power of eternal life and Yveltal's power of life absorption to once again take over their respective region. Then, one of the most recent events in Contemporary history is when we saw groups of people in the Galar Region try to re-summon Eternatus to spread energy throughout their lands. This caused Legendary Pokemon Zamazenta and Zacian to re-appear and once again, defeat Eternatus, putting an end to this rebellion.

Now, we are almost caught up to our present. There is currently an issue with time-traveling Pokemon in the Paldea region, but this is an on-going issue. This brief history is not meant to be a comprehensive history textbook, but rather a resource used to have a base-level understanding of how we got here today. Don't expect the Pokemon World to go back to thousands of years of order like we saw after the Darkest Day, there are still many chapters left to be written.
