After years and years of prosperity following The Darkest Day, there were a few other major events that lead us into our contemporary history. Around 1,500 years ago, the earliest known writing system is discovered replicating the Unknown alphabet. Unknown is a Pokemon that has 28 different forms: 26 for all the letters in the English alphabet, 1 representing the question mark, and 1 representing the exclamation point. Prior to this discovery, there were no confirmed or known writing systems that were used.

Approximately 700 years ago, the two legendary birds Lugia and Ho-Oh were discovered reseting upon a couple of different towers. These two birds had majestical powers not seen before. It was discovered that Lugia had the ability to control the weather and Ho-Oh had the ability to resurect other Pokemon. It is said that three Pokemon that died in a fire was resurected as the Legendary Beasts of Entei, Raikou, and Suicune.

One last major development occurs around 150 years ago. This is when Pokemon league competitions started to take off. The Pokemon leagues that we know of today began around this point in time. As Pokemon battling began to be a competitive pastime of many across the world, we would discover the first synthetic Pokemon known as Porygon. These events would lead us to our present where we would experience so many new trials and tribulations effectively ending thousands of years of peace and prosperity in the Pokemon universe.
